The Animi Beauty System® as a technique

The Animi Beauty System is not yoga, not meditation, not a relaxation technique. It is rather a technique to tune ourselves either when we are awake and conscious or when we are less awake. Possessing it makes us cheerful, calm and well-balanced and we are ready for any change. As a result you will realize that you actually have a huge role in your own feelings, the happenings of your life and the forming of your environment. You will not only realize it, but you will be able to make good use of it: Through yourself you can produce an effect on them.

The Animi Beauty System is very complex and diverse even though it is very simple. That makes it possible for everybody – just like you – to find themselves, their harmony and their own way. The Animi is in you either you see the world rationally or spiritually.

The Animi Beauty System® as a state

The Animi means ‘at heart’. During our Animi treatments everyone can experience the harmony and peace at heart. I first discovered this mental state in myself. Later I tried to call it consciously forth: it is a very happy feeling when you feel that ‘All is very well’, you are balanced with yourself and in harmony with the World. The chaotic conditions of the outer world won’t put an effect on you even if you are aware of their existence, you feel whole. That is what we call Animi.

As a practicing Animi Soul-Beautician I help you from my heart. Intuitions and intimations lead me to the state of Animi. This is the soul and essence of the Animi Beauty System: ‘You discover this feeling at your heart and experience its beneficial, healing, harmonizing, balancing power and we will teach you to easily get there anytime you wish.’ /Kriszta Halász/
